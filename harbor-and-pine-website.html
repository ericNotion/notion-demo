Coastal Simplicity, Thoughtfully Made

We create beautifully simple essentials for home and body—small-batch pieces crafted with intention, designed to last, and made with respect for our planet.

Our Story

Founded in 2019 in Portland, Oregon, Harbor & Pine was born from a simple belief: that the things we bring into our homes should be as thoughtful as they are beautiful. We're a small team of ~15 makers and dreamers committed to coastal-inspired living.

Our Values

We believe in coastal simplicity, sustainable sourcing, and small-batch craft. Every piece we create is designed to bring calm and intentionality to your daily rituals— from the moment you wake to the moment you rest.

Our Mission

In a world of excess, we champion intentional living. We create products that encourage you to buy less and buy better—choosing quality over quantity, and mindfulness over mass production.

Our Collections

Discover our thoughtfully curated collections, each piece designed to bring tranquility and beauty to your everyday moments.

🛏️

Home Textiles

Organic linen bedding, hand-woven throws, and cotton towels that get softer with every wash. Made from sustainably sourced natural fibers, each piece brings effortless elegance to your living spaces.

🏺

Handcrafted Goods

Small-batch ceramics, stoneware, and home accessories crafted by local artisans. Each piece is one-of-a-kind, celebrating the beauty of imperfection and the warmth of human touch.

🕯️

Scent & Bath

Botanical candles, natural soaps, and bath salts made with essential oils and plant-based ingredients. Transform your self-care routine into a moment of coastal calm and rejuvenation.

What Makes Us Different

Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and craftsmanship sets us apart in a world of fast fashion and disposable goods.

🌾

Materials-First Sourcing

We start with the finest natural materials—organic linen, hand-harvested botanicals, and sustainably sourced clay—ensuring every product is as kind to the earth as it is beautiful.

✋

Small-Batch Production

Every piece is made in limited quantities, allowing us to maintain the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship while minimizing waste and environmental impact.

♻️

Circular Philosophy

From biodegradable packaging to products designed to last a lifetime, we're committed to closing the loop and creating a more sustainable future for all.
