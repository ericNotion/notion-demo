Buy Less, Buy Better

Founded in 2019 in Portland, Oregon, Harbor & Pine was born from a simple belief: that the things we bring into our homes should be beautiful, functional, and kind to the planet. We partner with small-batch artisans and sustainable mills to create home goods that celebrate coastal simplicity and intentional living.

Every linen sheet is OEKO-TEX certified. Every candle is hand-poured. Every ceramic piece is one-of-a-kind. We're not chasing trends — we're building a collection of timeless essentials that get better with age.
